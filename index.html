About Us

Baked Fresh, Made to Celebrate

Welcome to Gourmet Fine Pastries. We specialise in custom celebration cakes, fresh daily pastries, and bakes for every occasion — from birthdays and weddings to parties and corporate events.

  • Custom celebration cakes designed around your event — birthdays, weddings, parties and corporate occasions.
  • Fresh pastries baked daily, from morning treats to afternoon indulgences.
  • Every order is made to order with the finest ingredients — never mass-produced.
  • Order in our app for pickup or delivery, with a simple 50% deposit to confirm your cake.
